KOTA is made up of talented creatives that take business seriously.

Our Mission

To make the digital world more beautiful, thoughtful and impactful.

Our mission is simple – Rebel against boring. There is no excuse for bad design, and there is no reason for any design to ever be dull. We believe in making the world a damn site better, through the power of beautiful, thoughtful and impactful design, and with an ethos that is colourful, conversational, and non-conformist.

LIFE 2.0

The New Normal

As an agency we have adapted to new ways of working that brings a better harmony to the work-life balance. Most of our roles are no-longer location dependent, meaning we attract talent and clients from all over the world. We’ve created a huge open-source Employee Handbook that explains all our policies, including flexible working from home and our supporting benefits that help enable the team to flourish.

As a group we meet regularly for team activities followed by a social event, with a summer trip and Christmas extravaganza being the highlights each year.

Our Clients

We work with established brands worldwide who are design and trend conscious.

The brands we partner with are looking to push their industry boundaries, and ready to invest quality resources into their digital. They want to work with a close, more specialist team they click with – and who they trust to take them outside of their creative comfort zone.

Our core strength is our creativity and concept. We have the design eye and expertise to build beautiful, bespoke sites and help brands stand out. Of course we care about our clients business growth, but they come to us for our artistic flair and creative reasoning.

Digital Designer

More Details

We’re on the lookout for an experienced Mid-weight/Senior Digital Designer to join our awesome, creative team.

Description

  • Lead digital design projects inc wireframing, prototyping, design and presenting to clients.
  • Working as part of the design team on wider branding projects.

Requirements

  • High level of proficiency in XD / Figma.

  • Branding and strategy experience is desirable.

About you

  • You have at least 3 years experience at a Digital design agency.
  • You are adaptable and forward thinking.
  • Efficient, detail-oriented, organised, and able to work under tight deadlines.
  • You can work on multiple projects at the same time.
  • You can provide awesome examples of your work.
  • Able to build personal relationships and trust with clients.
  • Great communication skills and confident leading own projects.

Benefits

  • Fully remote working.
  • Flexible working.
  • 20 days holiday + bank holidays + additional day per year of service + never work your birthday.
  • Yearly company trip and in person meetings.
  • An awesome team working across creative and forward-thinking projects.

Creative Front End Developer

More Details

We’re on the lookout for an experienced Mid-weight Creative Front End Developer to join our awesome team.

Description

  • Build industry leading websites for an exciting and diverse client range around the world.
  • Code websites from an Adobe XD design.
  • Providing time estimates to project managers.
  • Collaborating with design team.

Requirements

Personal skills:

  • Honesty and integrity.

  • Supportive and passionate team

  • Great working conditions with a relaxed and fun atmosphere
  • Forward-thinking company with a positive attitude
